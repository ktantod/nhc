About this Calculator

The kidney failure risk equations were developed in patients with CKD stages G3-G5 referred to nephrologists in Canada, and have now been validated in more than 700,000 individuals spanning 30+ countries worldwide.

The Alerts uses the four variable equation to predict the 5 year probability of treated kidney failure (dialysis or transplantation) for a potential patient with CKD Stage 3 to 5.

Online implementation of this calculator is here: https://kidneyfailurerisk.com/
